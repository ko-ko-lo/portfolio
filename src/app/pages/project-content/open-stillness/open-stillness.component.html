Why was this platform created?

What started as a personal solution for organizing my yoga routines has evolved into a full-fledged mindfulness platform. Initially, I struggled with scattered paper notes, which led me to develop a structured digital library for Yin-style Yoga poses and routines. As I refined the concept, the platform expanded to include guided Meditations, Breathwork techniques, and more.

As both the designer and developer, I have built this platform from the ground up - crafting the UI, structuring the architecture, and ensuring accessibility. More than just a coding project, this is a way to share my experience with mindfulness, making Meditation, Yin-Style Yoga, and Breathwork more accessible to others.

Project Phases:
The Evolution of Open Stillness

Every project starts with a simple idea. What began as a structured way to organize Yin-Style Yoga routines has grown into a comprehensive mindfulness platform. This journey is divided into key phases, each adding new features and refining the user experience. From the initial foundation to enhanced content, accessibility, and personalization, the platform continuously evolves to bring Meditation, Breathwork, and Yin-Style Yoga to a wider audience.

1

MVP / Walking Skeleton

Completed

The foundation of the platform was established in this phase. The architecture was carefully planned, the technology stack chosen, and the design language defined. A scalable design system was developed, and a walking skeleton was implemented using Angular to ensure a solid starting point for future expansions.

In Progress

Transforming the platform into a Progressive Web App (PWA) allows users to access mindfulness practices anytime, anywhere. Offline functionality, push notifications, and performance optimizations will be key focuses, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience even without an internet connection.
